5 Some Papers to Note

  • [1] “steers” a VLA by having it generate multiple action sequences, rolling them out in sim, and using a VLM to select the best.
  • [2] Uses Bayesian optimization to fit controller hyperparameters and (potentially incorrect) model parameters in order to maximize reward against the true model parameters. This is potentially related to ideas around the usefulness of robust control.

References

[1]
Y. Wu, A. Li, T. Hermans, F. Ramos, A. Bajcsy, and C. P’erez-D’Arpino, “Do what you say: Steering vision-language-action models via runtime reasoning-action alignment verification,” arXiv preprint arXiv:2510.16281, 2025.
[2]
R. Guzman, R. Oliveira, and F. Ramos, “Bayesian optimisation for robust model predictive control under model parameter uncertainty,” in 2022 international conference on robotics and automation (ICRA), 2022, pp. 5539–5545.
